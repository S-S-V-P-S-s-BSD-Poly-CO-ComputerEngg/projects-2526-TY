Policy

1. What documents do I need to rent a vehicle?

For self-drive rentals, you must provide a valid Original Driving License and a government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar Card or Passport). For chauffeur-driven rides, only a valid photo ID is required.

2. Is fuel included in the rental price?

No. GoWheels operates on a "Level-to-Level" fuel policy. You must return the vehicle with the same amount of fuel it had when you picked it up to avoid refuelling penalties.

3. How do security deposits work?

A refundable security deposit is held securely in your GoWheels Wallet during your trip. It is released back to your original payment method within 3-5 business days after a successful, damage-free vehicle return.

4. Can I cross state borders?

Yes, our vehicles possess All-India tourist permits. However, any state-specific entry taxes or border permits are the responsibility of the renter to pay at the toll checkpoints.

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing or using the GoWheels platform, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service. You must be at least 18 years old and possess a valid driving license to rent a self-drive vehicle.

2. Vehicle Use Rules

  • Vehicles must not be used for racing, towing, or off-road driving.
  • Smoking and consumption of alcohol inside the vehicle is strictly prohibited.
  • The renter is fully responsible for any traffic violations, parking fines, or toll charges incurred during the rental period.

3. Liability and Damages

You agree to return the vehicle in the same condition it was provided. GoWheels reserves the right to charge your wallet or payment method for any damages, excessive cleaning fees, or late return penalties.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our rental services, including your name, contact details, payment information, driving license copies, and location data during active trips for safety and tracking purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to verify your identity, process transactions, provide customer support, and improve our services. We do not sell your personal information to third-party marketers.

3. Data Security

GoWheels employs industry-standard AES-256 encryption to protect your sensitive data. Payment processing is handled by secure, PCI-DSS compliant third-party gateways (e.g., Stripe, Razorpay).

1. Booking Cancellations

All bookings made on GoWheels are final. Due to the logistical nature of vehicle dispatching, cancellations are not permitted once a booking is confirmed.

2. Security Deposit Refunds

Security deposits are initiated for refund immediately after the vehicle is returned and inspected. Please allow 3 to 5 business days for the funds to reflect in your bank account or credit card statement.

3. Exceptions

In the rare event that GoWheels is unable to provide the booked vehicle due to maintenance issues or prior accidents, a full 100% refund (including rental fees) will be issued instantly to your GoWheels Wallet or original payment method.

1. Standard Coverage

All GoWheels rentals include basic comprehensive insurance and third-party liability coverage. This protects you against major financial loss in the event of an unavoidable accident.

2. Renter Deductible (Liability)

In the event of damage to the GoWheels vehicle, the renter is liable to pay a standard deductible (usually up to ₹10,000 depending on the vehicle class). Damage costs exceeding this deductible are covered by our insurance.

3. Accident Protocol

If an accident occurs, you must immediately notify GoWheels Support and file a local police report (FIR) if a third party is involved. Failure to report an accident immediately may void the insurance coverage.
