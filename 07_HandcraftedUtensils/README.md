## рџ“Њ Description
Songir village in Dhule district is famous for its traditional handcrafted brass and copper utensils made by skilled local artisans. These utensils are an important part of the regionвЂ™s cultural and economic heritage.

However, most products are sold only through local markets, which limits their reach. To solve this, the **Songir Handcrafted Utensils Portal** is developed.
